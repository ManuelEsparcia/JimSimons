MODULE: decile_analysis.py
VERSION: MVP Week 5

OBJECTIVE
- Evaluate whether daily signal ranking separates realized outcomes by bucket/decile.

INPUTS
- `data/signals/signals_daily.parquet`
- `data/labels/labels_forward.parquet`

OUTPUTS
- `data/signals/decile_daily.parquet`
- `data/signals/decile_summary.parquet`
- `data/signals/decile_analysis_summary.json`
- parquet manifests alongside outputs.

TEMPORAL SEMANTICS
- Joins by `date, instrument_id, label_name` (+ `horizon_days` when available).
- Uses label `label_value` as realized target (no intraperiod path modeling).
- Works on selected split roles (default `valid,test`).

DESIGN DECISIONS
- Reports per-date per-bucket mean/median target and counts.
- Computes top-minus-bottom spread per date and aggregate statistics.
- Monotonicity score uses correlation between bucket index and mean target.
- Fail-fast on ambiguous model/label/horizon combinations.

MVP LIMITS
- No transaction costs or PnL attribution.
- No bootstrap/statistical significance testing.
- No subuniverse stratification.
