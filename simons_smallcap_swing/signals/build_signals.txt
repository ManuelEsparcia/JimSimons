MODULE: build_signals.py
VERSION: MVP Week 5

OBJECTIVE
- Convert OOS model predictions into deterministic cross-sectional daily signals.

INPUTS
- Predictions artifact (`*_predictions.parquet`) with at least:
  `date, instrument_id, ticker, split_role, label_name` and score field (`prediction` or `pred_proba`).
- `universe_history.parquet` for eligibility filtering (`date, instrument_id, is_eligible`) when enabled.

OUTPUTS
- `data/signals/signals_daily.parquet`
- `data/signals/signals_daily.summary.json`
- parquet manifest alongside output.

TEMPORAL SEMANTICS
- Operates on prediction rows already aligned to split roles.
- Default filter keeps `valid,test`; train rows are excluded unless explicitly requested.
- No future merge beyond same-date eligibility check.

DESIGN DECISIONS
- `instrument_id` is the primary identity; ticker is attribute.
- Per-date ranking with deterministic tie-break (`raw_score desc`, `instrument_id asc`).
- Produces `raw_score`, `rank_pct`, `bucket`, `signal_side`, `is_top`, `is_bottom`.
- Supports regression and classification score sources.

MVP LIMITS
- No blending/ensemble layer.
- No sector/subuniverse ranking.
- No dynamic top-N sizing.
