MODULE: paper_portfolio.py
VERSION: MVP Week 5

OBJECTIVE
- Build a simple paper portfolio from ranked signals and realized forward labels.

INPUTS
- `data/signals/signals_daily.parquet`
- `data/labels/labels_forward.parquet`

OUTPUTS
- `data/signals/paper_portfolio_daily.parquet`
- `data/signals/paper_portfolio_positions.parquet`
- `data/signals/paper_portfolio_summary.json`
- parquet manifests alongside outputs.

TEMPORAL SEMANTICS
- Uses `label_value` as realized return proxy after join on
  `date, instrument_id, label_name` (+ `horizon_days` when available).
- Split-role filter defaults to `valid,test`.
- No look-ahead transformations after label materialization.

DESIGN DECISIONS
- Supported modes:
  - `long_only_top`
  - `long_short_top_bottom`
  - `short_only_bottom` (optional)
- Equal-weight per side:
  - long: `+1/N_long`
  - short: `-1/N_short`
- Contribution check enforced:
  `sum(contribution) == gross_portfolio_return` per date/mode.
- Explicit skip handling for missing sides (`missing_long`, `missing_short`, etc.).

MVP LIMITS
- No costs, slippage, borrow, turnover, or optimizer.
- No risk constraints or advanced position sizing.
- Not a full backtest engine.
