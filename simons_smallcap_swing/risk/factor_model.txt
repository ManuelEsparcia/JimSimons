\section{Objetivo y Alcance}
Modelo factorial para exposures \(\beta\), factor returns \(f_t\), specific risk \(\sigma_\epsilon\), cov \(\Sigma = B \Omega_f B^T + D\). Soporta small caps swings con alta idiosincrasia $[web:25]$.

\section{Teoría Matemática (APT)}
\[
r_{i,t} = \beta_{i,t}^T f_t + \epsilon_{i,t}, \quad \Cov(r) = B \Omega_f B^T + D
\]
- \(B\): Exposures rolling OLS, lookback 252d half-life.
- \(f_t\): Cross-sectional reg \( r_{i,t} = \alpha_t + \beta_t f_t + \epsilon \).
- \(\sigma_{\epsilon_i}\): EWMA residuals \( \sigma^2_t = \lambda \sigma^2_{t-1} + (1-\lambda) e_t^2 \).
- Shrinkage \(\Omega_f\): Ledoit-Wolf.
