\section{Teoría Matemática}
Stressed PnL: Perturba \( r \to r + \Delta \), cov \(\Sigma \to (1+\kappa) \Sigma + \Delta\rho\).

ES:
\[
ES_\alpha = -\E[r | r \le q_\alpha], \quad q_\alpha = \inf \{ q: P(r \le q) \ge \alpha \}
\]

Copula shock: \( C(u_1,...,u_n) \) para joint tails (Clayton para lower).

Liquidity impact: \( \text{Price}_t = P_0 + \lambda \cdot \frac{V_t}{\sqrt{ADV_t}} \).
