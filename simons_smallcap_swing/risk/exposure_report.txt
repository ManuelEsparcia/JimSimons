\section{Objetivo y Alcance}
Reporta exposures factoriales/sectoriales/concentración/liquidez para monitoreo diario y gates pre-trade. Limita beta<1.2, sector<20\%, HHI<0.1, participation<3\% en swings $[web:25]$.

\section{Teoría Matemática}
Factor exposure (APT):
\[
e_f = \sum_i w_i \beta_{i,f}, \quad r_i = \alpha_i + \sum_f \beta_{i,f} f + \epsilon_i
\]
Gross/Net: $Gross = \sum |w_i|$, $Net = \sum w_i$.

HHI:
\[
HHI = \sum_i w_i^2, \quad \text{cap } HHI \le 0.15
\]

Liquidez:
\[
DTL = \frac{|w_i| \cdot Price_i}{\lambda \cdot ADV_i}, \quad \lambda=0.1\% \text{ (impact)}
\]

Participation: $ \frac{Trade\ Notional}{ADV} < 5\% $.

\section{Análisis del Flujo}
\begin{enumerate}
\item Load positions, factor_exposures (parquet PIT), sector_map.
\item Enrich: $e_f = w \cdot \beta$.
\item Compute: gross/net, sector sums, top5 weights, HHI, DTL/participation.
\item Limits eval: breaches por severity.
\item Persist: daily.parquet, summary.json, breaches.parquet.
\end{enumerate}

Casos: Fallback 0 en missing \(\beta\), warn ilíquidos.
