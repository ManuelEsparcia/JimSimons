\section{Objetivo y Alcance}
Monitoreo real-time/backtest de drawdown para proteger capital en swings adversos. Reglas: warning (-8\%), de-risk (-12\%), kill (-15\%), staged re-entry. Integra con cov\_shrinkage.py para risk holístico $[web:42]$.

\section{Teoría Matemática}
Drawdown:
\[
DD_t = \frac{NAV_t - HWM_t}{HWM_t}, \quad HWM_t = \max_{s \le t} NAV_s
\]
Optimal control (Kelly fractional):
\[
f_t^* = f_K \cdot (1 + \beta DD_t), \quad f_K = \frac{\mu_p}{\sigma_p^2}
\]
Donde \(\beta <0\) reduce sizing en DD. Calmar: \( C = \frac{\text{Return}/\text{Year}}{|\text{MaxDD}|} \).

CVaR trigger: De-risk si 
\[
\text{CVaR}_\alpha = -\frac{1}{\alpha} \int_0^\alpha VaR_u \, du > \theta
\]

\section{Análisis del Flujo del Módulo}
\begin{enumerate}
\item Carga NAV/PnL (parquet).
\item HWM rolling max, $DD_t = (NAV_t / HWM_t) - 1$.
\item Check tiers: warning/de-risk/kill.
\item Acciones: reduce positions 50-100\%, cooldown days.
\item Persist: series.parquet, actions.parquet, summary.json (MaxDD, events).
\end{enumerate}

Manejo: NAV>0, DD \in [-1,0]. CLI para backtest.

\section{Optimizaciones SOTA Swing Trading}
- Thresholds HMM-regime: Alta vol → tight (-10/-15/-20\%).
- Bayesian: $P(DD > \theta | data) > 0.1$ → trigger.
- Vol targeting: $f_t = target\_vol / \sqrt{w^T \Sigma_{shrink} w}$.
