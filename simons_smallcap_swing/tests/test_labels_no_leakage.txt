\section{Objetivo Estratégico}
Asegurar labels forward/neutralizados sin fuga temporal ni misalignment con features/splits. Vital para swing: labels $y_t = r_{t:t+h}$ ($h=5$días) deben ser estrictamente futuro, maximizando IC OOS $\corr(\pred_t, y_t) > 0.20$ sin lookahead. Pipeline protegido como en Two Sigma/RenTech.[web:54][web:56]

\section{Casos Mínimos y Assertions Temporales}
\begin{itemize}
\item \texttt{test\_forward\_label\_uses\_only\_future\_window\_configured}: $y_t \in [t+1, t+h]$.
\item \texttt{test\_last\_horizon\_rows\_marked\_invalid}: Filas $t > T-h$ tagged NaN.
\item \texttt{test\_decision\_lag\_alignment\_features\_labels}: $\features_t$ alinea $\decision\_ts = t$.
\item \texttt{test\_neutralized\_targets\_reduce\_factor\_corr}: $\corr(y^{neu}, f) < \epsilon$.
\item \texttt{test\_purged\_splits\_remove\_overlap}: No overlap train/test post-purge.
\end{itemize}

\section{Matemáticas Avanzadas: Labels Forward y Neutralización}
Forward return: $y_t^{(h)} = \frac{P_{t+h} - P_t}{P_t}$, o log $r = \log(P_{t+h}/P_t)$. Last rows: $\I(t \leq T-h)$. Neutralizaci\'on: $y^{neu} = y - \beta \cdot F$, $\beta = \argmin \E[(y - \beta F)^2]$, reduce $\corr(y,F) \approx 0$. Purged split: Train $[t_0, t_1 - T_{emb}]$, $T_{emb}=h+m$. Meta-labeling: $\bar{y}_t = \sign(\pred_t - \threshold)$.[web:54][web:45]
