Gestionar rutas deterministas para reproducibilidad total:
\begin{equation}
\text{Path}(D,E,T,R) = \text{root} + f(D,E,T,R) \quad \forall D,E,T,R
\end{equation}
donde $f$ es función hash determinista, garantizando zero ambiguity.

\section{Path Safety Industrial}
\begin{lstlisting}
def validate_path(path: Path, allowed_root: Path) -> bool:
    """Path confinement: reject jailbreaks"""
    return (path.resolve().is_relative_to(allowed_root.resolve()) 
            and ".." not in path.parts)
\end{lstlisting}
