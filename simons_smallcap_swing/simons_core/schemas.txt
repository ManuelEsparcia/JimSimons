\section{Objetivo y Rol Crítico}
Centraliza schemas para \texttt{prices\_raw} $\to$ \texttt{predictions} $\to$ \texttt{positions}, con PK (date,symbol) y metadata (run\_id). Previene garbage-in-garbage-out: +25\% Sharpe en backtests validados \cite{lopez_data}.

\section{Alcance y Schemas Clave}
- \texttt{prices\_adjusted\_schema}: date (date), symbol (str), open/high/low/close (f64), volume (i64).
- \texttt{features\_schema}: date,symbol + zscore\_mom (f64), vol\_regime (cat).
- \texttt{labels\_schema}: future\_ret (f64), binary\_swing (bool).
- \texttt{predictions\_schema}: prob\_long (f64[0,1]), confidence (f64).

Reglas: Nullable solo volume, PK unique.

\section{Matemáticas de Validación de Schemas}
\subsection{Tipos Algebraicos y Constraints}
Schema como tipo dependiente: $S ::= \Sigma_{c \in cols} Type_c \times Nullable_c$.
Uniqueness PK:
\[
\forall (d,s) \in PK, \, |\{df | date=d \land symbol=s\}| = 1
\]
Outlier gate: $x_i \in [\mu \pm 5\sigma]$ o flag \cite{robust}.

\subsection{Data Drift Detection}
KS-test entre runs:
\[
D = \sup |F_1(x) - F_2(x)| < 0.1
\]
Para features swing \cite{stockformer}.
